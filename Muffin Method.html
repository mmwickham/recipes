Mixing with the Muffin Method

The Muffin Method

The Muffin Method

This is for loose, crumbly baked goods. It is also one of the easier mixing methods and great way for kids to be introduced to baking.

Need to make muffins, brownies, cakes or quickbreads? This is how you make them.
  1. Add all dry ingredients to a large bowl.
  2. Whisk to combine dry ingredients.
  3. In a separate, medium bowl, mix wet ingredients together.
  4. Ensure the wet ingredients are thoroughly mixed.
  5. Create a well in the dry ingredients with a mixing spoon.
  6. Pour wet ingredients into well you have created.
  7. Using a spoon or rubber spatula, mix wet and dry ingredients.
  8. Mix until all the ingredients are just combined.

    Notes:

    Be sure to sift flour before adding the flour to the bowl.

    Butter and/or oil and sugar are considere wet ingredients.

    If using butter, make sure butter is melted.

    Do not over mix the dough. Lump of flour are okay.
