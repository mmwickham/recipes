Chocolate Crinkle Cookies

Ingredients

Ingredients

For the cookies
½ cup brown sugar
½ cup butter
1 large egg
1 teaspoon vanilla
1 cup flour
½ cup plus 2 tablespoons cocoa powder
1 teaspoon baking soda
¼ teaspoon salt
¾ cup dark chocolate chips
1 cup powdered sugar
Steps

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°.
  2. Mix cookies according to creaming method.
  3. Refriderate cookies for at least 2 hours, up to overnight.
  4. Dish out cookies in ping-pong sized balls.
  5. Roll cookie ball into powdered sugar.
  6. Place cookie balls on ungreased cookie sheet, bake for 10 to 12 minutes.
  7. Makes about 20 cookies.
